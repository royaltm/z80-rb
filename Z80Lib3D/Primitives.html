    SinCos

    A Z80::Utils::SinCos table entry struct.

    Consists of two words:

    • sin

    • cos

    where each word is a 16-bit fixed point, twos complement signed number with the integral part in its high 8 bits and the fractional part in its low 8 bits, in the range: [-1.0, 1.0].
